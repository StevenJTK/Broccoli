Beef & Broccoli

Recipe

Beef & Broccoli is a healthy staple that has a complete balance of nutrients and tastes amazing! You will find the ingredients list below this recipe. Begin by whisking your oyster sauce, sherry, sesame oil, soy sauce, sugar and cornstarch into a bowl. Do this until the sugar has dissolved into the mix. Next, place your steak in a shallow bowl, pouring the oyster sauce onto the meat. Stir to coat your beef and let marinade for about 30 minutes in your fridge. Now the fun part, heat up your oil of choice (we recommend vegetable oil) in preferably a wok, if not, a large skillet will do, over medium-high heat. Add your ginger & garlic and let sizzle for about a minute to flavor your oil. Remove and discard after this process. Stir in your broccoli, tossing until bright green and close to tenderness. This should take you between 5 to 7 minutes. Remove the broccoli and set it aside. If needed, add more oil to the wok and start frying your beef that has been marinating in your fridge. Stir and toss until the sauce thickens and the beef is no longer pink. This will take about 5 minutes. Add your broccoli back in, stirring until it's warm, for about 3 minutes. Plate up and enjoy!

  • 1/3 cup of oyster sauce
  • 1/3 sherry
  • 2 teaspoons Asian (toasted) sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch
  • 3/4 pound beef round steak, cut into 1/8 inch thick strips
  • 3 tablespoons of vegetable oil or more
  • 1 thin slice of fresh ginger root
  • 1 clove garlic, peeled and smashed
  • 1 pound broccoli, cut into florets
